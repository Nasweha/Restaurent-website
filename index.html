OUR STORY

Here’s a true story about how, over two decades, BIG BITE customers created a new and pioneering food place. Thank you to them for their thousands of emails, letters, texts and phone calls along the way. I first visited Tokyo in the winter of ‘94 and fell in love with the harmony and precision of the food; every bento box was a work of art. If beautiful food, fast, was humanly possible, then Japan was leading the way, and it’s probably why nearly 100,000 people there are over one hundred years old. I returned home determined to create a new kind of food place...
